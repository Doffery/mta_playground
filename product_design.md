## Phase 0 – Internal Prototype (Your V1, but sharper)

Goal: Prove that multi-profile debate + synthesis is genuinely better than a single profile.

CLI or notebook is fine.

Invest heavily in:

Scaffolding for profiles

Conversation protocol

Synthesis format

Outputs you review yourself:

“Would I actually use this to inform a trade?”

“Is the disagreement interesting or just noise?”

👉 Ship to yourself in days, iterate quickly.

## Phase 1 – Public Alpha Web App (Your V2)

Goal: Validate user value and willingness to use in a real workflow.

Simple web app:

Input: ticker + timeframe

Select profiles

Analyze

Output:

Final verdict (with confidence level)

Where the profiles agree vs disagree

High-level debate (short)

Key metrics:

Do they run multiple analyses per session?

Do they come back weekly?

Which profiles are used most?

If you wanted, you could already charge here ($10–$20/m for early adopters).

## Phase 2 – Grounded Analysis (Slimmed V3 + seed of V5)

Goal: Move from “smart opinions” to “evidence-backed reasoning” without blowing up complexity.

Introduce shared fact base:

Pull key financial metrics

Pull recent news headlines

Normalize them into a compact “Fact Sheet”

Have all profiles reason on top of that.

UI:

“Facts used in this analysis”

Click to expand sources and numbers

This unlocks:

Better trust

Better fit for prosumers and analysts

Justification for higher pricing

## Phase 3 – Pro Mode: Deep Research + Monitoring

Goal: Build something professionals can live in.

“Deep Dive” analyses with:

Longer research

Citation-heavy reasoning

Ongoing monitoring:

“Re-run the analysis when:

new earnings come out

stock moves > X%

specific keywords show up in news”

Saveable “Analyst Profile Configs”:

Users tweak or build their own profiles

This becomes the start of your profile marketplace later

## Phase 4 – Content & Distribution (Your V4 podcast)

Goal: Growth and brand.

Auto-generate:

Short “podcast-style” conversations

Clips for social showing analysts debating

Tie back to product:

“This was generated by [Your App]. Try your own topic.”

This is your top-of-funnel, not core utility.
